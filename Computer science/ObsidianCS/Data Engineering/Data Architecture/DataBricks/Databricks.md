## Benefits of [[Databricks]]
* The core architecture is [[Lakehouse]]
* Has the both advantages from [[Data Lake]] and [[Data Warehouse]]
* It's [[multicloud]]
* Support [[Python]] 

## Difference between [[Databricks]] and [[Snowflake]]

* [[Snowflake]] its a [[SaaS]] that help us to store and manage files and data from another [[Cloud]], this is a good tool for simple and final customers
* [[Databricks]] also allow us to query, store and manage the data but the main purpose its the [[Data Architecture]] that allows create [[AI]] models, [[Data Clearing]] and another powerful steps
