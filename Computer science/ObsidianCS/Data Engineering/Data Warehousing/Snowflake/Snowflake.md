**What is snowflake?**
It is SaaS to manage [[Data Warehouse]] [[multicloud]]. It functions as a centralized "AI Data Cloud" where organizations store, analyze, and share massive amounts of structured and semi-structured data to power business intelligence

## Advantages
* Not software or Hardware provisioning needed
* Support task with many and different clouds
* [[Data Governance]] to force "least-privilige access" - Just access for the people who need the data
* Data Marketplace - We can use data from anothers dataset or companies in Snowflake
